By including input/_input.scss and input/_form.scss from hgrid, forms in your project will will look decent out of the box.

The signal colors used for checkboxes, radio buttons, buttons and focus outline can be set in the global variables. The styles are slightly opinionated, but conservative, css-only and respectful of browser functionality. For additional styling you can add utility classes such as radius-* for border-radius, mt-* for margin-top, bg-gray-* for background color, .block for display:block. Pseudo-selectors like :disabled and :readonly will automatically be reflected visually.
